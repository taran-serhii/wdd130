Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to introduce Stephen King's series of books called The Dark Tower to people, who never read or heard about it, and invite them to start this amazing journey. Additionaly, the website will invite those who already read the whole series to revisit it by reading additional materials and books, which are connected to the The Dark Tower series, but are not a part of the original 8-book set.

Audience

The main audience of this website are the people, who didn't yet read the The Dark Tower series or just started. The smaller part of audience are those who read the series, but wants to know more or to simply revisit it.
